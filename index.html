Colombia Unfiltered

From myth to reality. A country beyond Escobar.

About Me

I am from South America, Colombia, and I love my country deeply. My name is Susana Zapata, and I created this blog to change the mistaken perception that many people abroad still have about Colombia. I want to show the truth: the colours, the diversity, the resilience, and the transformation of a nation that is so much more than the shadow of Pablo Escobar. Welcome to a different Colombia.

Politics

Colombia's political landscape has evolved significantly in recent years. From peace accords to shifts in leadership, the country has taken important steps toward democratic strengthening, inclusion and transitional justice.

Nature

Colombia is one of the most biodiverse countries in the world. From the Amazon rainforest to the Andes mountains, its landscapes are a true natural treasure.

Coffee

Colombian coffee is world-renowned not only for its flavour but also for the culture around it. Coffee farms are a symbol of tradition, sustainability, and family heritage.

Cities

From the innovation of Medellín to the capital energy of Bogotá, Colombia’s cities blend modernity, culture, and history with resilience and vibrant life.

Colombia: 90s vs Now

Colombia in the 90s was marked by violence and conflict, but today, it has transformed remarkably. Innovation, art, tourism and hope now lead the narrative.

The Future of Colombia

The future of Colombia is filled with promise. With a new generation driving change, and a growing focus on sustainability, equality and education, Colombia is writing a new chapter—one of unity and potential.
